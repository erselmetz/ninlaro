Minarik et al. Czech RMG Real-world study:
a prospective analysis of Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd vs Rd in RRMM1

STUDY DESIGN:

A prospective analysis of the survival benefit
of Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd vs Rd in 344 patients
with RRMM treated in routine clinical practice in the Czech Republic1

At data cut-off*, median follow-up was 20.8 months
in the Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd cohort and 15.5
months in the Rd cohort1

Patients had received a median of 1 prior line
of treatment, the same as in the TOURMALINE-
MM1 study1,2

STUDY ENDPOINTS:

Primary endpoint was PFS; secondary endpoints included response rates and OS1

Study limitations: Real-world analyses are often nonrandomised, observational, retrospective studies that may have unobserved
treatment selection biases as well as other limitations that should be considered when comparing results with clinical trials.
Outcomes should be interpreted with caution due to small sample size, limited follow-up, and limited maturity of data.1
