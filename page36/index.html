With extended PFS and tolerability supported by Real-world studies, consider all-oral Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd for your appropriate patients with RRMM1–4

EXTENDED EFFICACY

Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd demonstrated extended PFS vs placebo + Rd in the TOURMALINE-MM1 RCT, which is replicated in Realworld studies1–4

mPFS in the ITT population: 20.6 months vs 14.7 months (HR=0.74, P=0.01)

mPFS in patients with high-risk cytogenetics: 21.7 months vs 9.7 months (HR=0.54)

MANAGEABLE TOLERABILITY

The tolerability of Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd has been demonstrated through clinical trials and Real-world evidence1,2,5

CONVENIENT ALL-ORAL, PI-BASED REGIMEN

Ixazomib (NINLARO) is the first and only PI with the convenience of oral administration6
