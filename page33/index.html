CLINICAL STUDY TITLE:

MM1: Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd was General Well-tolerated with Manageable Side Effects


No. of Patients: 361


Trial Design: Real-World Design


Clinical Endpoints:
Rates of discontinuation of the full study regimen due to AEs were 17% with Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd and 14% with placebo + Rd
Rates of dose reduction of any study drug due to AEs were 56% with Ixazomib (NINLARO) + Rd vs 50% with placebo + Rd.
